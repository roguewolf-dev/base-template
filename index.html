intro - projekt fOSS

welcome to projekt fOSS, a iniative for a Free & Open Source Society. projekt fOSS is a 100% student driven society, trying to revive the learning for new generation programmers & helping everyone get industry-ready



what's are we trying to do?

the academic approach of information technology & computer science engineering has become about mugging up Data Structures and Algorithms, or theoretical knowledge of decades old devices & softwares.

we aim to help everyone understand new stuffs, expand knowledge, collaborate with others, get skilled, build real-world projekts, and a lot more. to accomplish all of this we need some like minded partners for our journey so join us, join the peer group, let's help each other to create.learn.explore.



just another club?

so, there's already a bunch of clubs, and as cause of the pandemic, lockdown gave birth to several clubs & societies, just like us. though clubs & society are a great way to build yourself, and also yes we are like just other club, ekdum same2same. even we'll be trying to collaborate with them too.

but, as most of them are governed or backed by some major organisation, or have different branches, we are going to be 100% student driven, also we are a UnOfficial Society of Shri Shankracharya Technical Campus, Bhilai.



how to join?

for having a constant contact & a on-going discussion & doubt, issues and problem solving, we are using the Discord, as our primary platorm.

you have a lot of different categories & channels for different purposes & fields, join & learn with like minded peeps, join here



programs & contests

being a completely independent and 100% student driven, we can host any program or competition as per our wish, respecting end sem examinations or tests, or any occassion.

also, we have already planned alots of contests & programs for everyone, stay tuned!

also, Registrations for 'Pirates of Web' contest is going to start soon, and is scheduled to be organised this March, 2021
